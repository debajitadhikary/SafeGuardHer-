Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013

The "Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013" is an important piece of legislation in India aimed at preventing and addressing sexual harassment of women at workplaces. The Act was enacted to create a safe and respectful working environment for women and ensure that they can work free from the fear of sexual harassment.

The Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act, 1956

The Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act, 1956, is an Indian law aimed at combating human trafficking and sexual exploitation, with provisions for prevention, rescue, and rehabilitation of victims.

The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006

The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006, is an Indian law that prohibits child marriages and establishes mechanisms for prevention, protection, and annulment of such marriages, safeguarding the rights and well-being of minors.

Indian Penal Code (IPC) Amendments

Various amendments to the IPC have been made to address crimes against women, such as rape, sexual assault, and acid attacks, with stricter penalties for offenders.
